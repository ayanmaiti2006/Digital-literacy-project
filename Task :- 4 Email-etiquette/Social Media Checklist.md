### Task 4: Social Media Checklist

As a Digital Ambassador, I have compiled this checklist to help students maintain a positive and professional digital footprint.

#### ✅ The Do's
1. **Privacy Check:** Regularly update security settings to limit access to personal data.
2. **Professional Networking:** Use platforms to showcase academic projects and connect with industry experts.
3. **Respectful Engagement:** Always use polite and inclusive language in comments and threads.
4. **Fact-Checking:** Verify the authenticity of news or links before hitting the share button.
5. **Impact Awareness:** Consider if a post aligns with the professional image you want to project to recruiters.

#### ❌ The Don'ts
1. **Offensive Content:** Avoid posting anything that could be seen as hate speech, bullying, or harassment.
2. **Over-Sharing:** Never post sensitive data like your registration number, address, or OTPs.
3. **Reactive Posting:** Do not post in anger; wait until you are calm to respond to online conflicts.
4. **Unauthorized Tagging:** Don't upload or tag photos of peers without asking for their consent first.
5. **Plagiarism:** Don't share someone else's creative work or research without giving proper credit.
